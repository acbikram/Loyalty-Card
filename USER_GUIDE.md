# User Guide

Welcome to **Universal Loyalty Wallet** — all your loyalty cards in one place, on
your device, offline.

## Getting started
1. Open the app — no sign-up, no account.
2. Add your first card (see below).
3. Optionally turn on **App Lock** and pick your **language** in Settings.

## Adding a card
- **Scan:** tap **Add → Scan**, point the camera at the card's barcode/QR. The app
  detects the symbology automatically.
- **From an image:** import a photo of the card; the app reads the code.
- **Manually:** type the number and pick the store and barcode type.
The app warns you if a card looks like a **duplicate**.

## Showing a card at checkout
- Open the card to display a large, scannable barcode/QR. Brightness is maximised
  for easy scanning. You can also copy the number.

## Organising
- **Favourite** and **pin** the cards you use most so they're on top.
- **Categories** (Supermarket, Pharmacy, Fuel, …) and **search** help you find
  cards fast.
- **Archive** or **hide** cards you rarely use to declutter.

## Search
- Type a store, nickname, or number; results rank by relevance with typo
  tolerance. Filter by category and re-sort by name or store.

## Security
- **App Lock:** biometric, PIN, or device credential. Set an idle timeout and
  lock-on-background.
- **Screenshot protection:** optionally block screenshots and the app-switcher
  preview.
- Your PIN is never stored — only a secure hash.

## Backup, restore & import
- **Backup/Export:** create a JSON or CSV export, optionally **password-encrypted**,
  saved wherever you choose.
- **Import wizard:** bring in a JSON/encrypted backup, a CSV, or a card image.
  Preview first, resolve duplicates, and **undo** if needed.
- The app never uploads anything by itself.

## Widgets
- Add the **Quick Scan** or **Favourite Card** widget to your home screen for
  one-tap access.

## Language & accessibility
- Choose **English** or **العربية**; the layout mirrors (RTL) for Arabic. On
  Android 13+ you can also set the app's language from system Settings.
- Works with **TalkBack** and large fonts; a **reduced-motion** option is
  available.

## Notifications (optional)
- Turn on reminders (e.g. backup reminders) in Settings. Android 13+ asks for
  permission first.

## Your data & privacy
- Everything stays on your device. Deleting a card removes it immediately;
  uninstalling removes all app data. See `PRIVACY.md`.

## Tips
- Add a **nickname** to tell similar cards apart.
- Keep a recent **encrypted backup** so a new phone is a quick restore.
